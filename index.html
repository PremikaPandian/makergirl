About Us

MakerGirl is a program led by University of Illinois students that educates 7-10 year old girls on STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) fields through 3-D printing workshops at the College of Business MakerLab, creative activities, and lessons on leading women in STEM fields.

Vision

There will be gender equality in number, pay, and expectation in all STEM workplaces.

Mission

By 2025, there will be an equal number of women and men involved in STEM K-12 education programs and pursuing STEM degrees.
